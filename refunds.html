Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

At Shumba Creations & Logistics, we value transparency. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how cancellations and refunds are handled for our services.

1. General Policy

All service requests are confirmed only after full or agreed payment has been received.

Our 10% service fee for sourcing and logistics is non-refundable once work has started.

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed to the original payment method within 7–14 business days.

2. Design Services (Websites, Posters, Documents)

Before work begins: Full refund is available if cancellation is requested before design work has started.

After drafts/deliverables are shared: Payments become non-refundable, as work has already been performed.

Revisions: Minor revisions are included as stated in the plan. Additional revisions may incur extra charges.

3. Sourcing & Procurement Services

Once we have engaged suppliers, negotiated, or arranged inspections, service fees are non-refundable.

If a client cancels before supplier contact has been made, a partial refund (minus administrative charges) may be available.

4. Logistics & Freight Services

Freight bookings are subject to carrier and port rules.

If canceled by client: Any cancellation fees charged by carriers will be deducted from the refund.

If canceled by carrier or due to force majeure: We will assist in rebooking, but refunds are subject to the carrier’s policy.

5. Document & Translation Services

Before work begins: Full refund available.

After work has started or been delivered: No refunds.

6. Exceptions

No refunds will be provided in cases where:

  • The client provides incorrect or incomplete shipment/document details.
  • Customs clearance is denied due to client’s non-compliance.
  • Delays are caused by carriers, customs, or force majeure events.

7. Contact for Refund Requests

All refund or cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to: shumbacreations@outlook.com or via WhatsApp at +8613779964069.

By engaging our services, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.
