Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

At Shumba Creations & Logistics, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you request a quote or contact us.
  • Service Details: Shipment details, product specifications, design requirements, and any files you provide.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited and interactions with our website (collected via standard web logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage our services, including quoting, sourcing, logistics coordination, and design work.
  • To communicate with you about orders, service updates, and marketing where you have consented.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers (freight forwarders, payment processors, or suppliers) strictly to fulfill your requests. Any third party we use will be required to protect your data.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

5. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have concerns about security, contact us directly.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at shumbacreations@outlook.com.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country, including in China. By using our services, you consent to these transfers.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the updated policy on this page with the revised effective date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at shumbacreations@outlook.com or WhatsApp at +8613779964069.
